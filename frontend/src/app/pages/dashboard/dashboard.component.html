Never Share OTP or PINs

No legitimate bank, Easypaisa, or JazzCash employee will ever ask for your 4-digit PIN, OTP (One-Time Password) or log-in passcode. Keep these confidential under all circumstances.

language

Inspect URLs Carefully

Phishing links mimic official names but have domain errors (e.g. jazzcash.fake-verify.com instead of jazzcash.com.pk). Always inspect the domain prefix on your browser.

volunteer_activism

Double Check Cash Prize Offers

Lottery awards claiming you won thousands (e.g. from BISP or TV Shows) are scam tactics. They will request money first for "registration fee". Legitimate programs never ask for registration fees to cash rewards.
